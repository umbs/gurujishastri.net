Guruji Pradeep Shastri

Born in a small village in Bihar, India, He left home at an early age to obtain education from various ashram in Himalayas.

Over the past 40 years, He has been teaching Yoga and conducting lectures on Hinduism, Vedas, Upanishads, Bhagavad Gita etc.

He has conducted seminars in most part of the world like Thailand, Singapore, Japan, New Zealand, Australia, Tahiti (French Polynesia), France, Belgium, Switzerland, Fiji, Canada and USA.

He brings vast knowledge, great depth and new perspective to age old scriptures.
